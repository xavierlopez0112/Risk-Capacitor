Title
Risk Capacitor: Algorithmic Stock Analysis & Risk Modeling

Project Summary

A quantitative finance toolkit analyzing stocks by combining:

Sentiment Analysis of financial news
Regression Models: Polynomial, Cubic Spline, B-Spline
Stochastic Simulations: Monte Carlo + GARCH
Risk Metrics: VaR, CVaR, Max Drawdown, Sharpe/Sortino ratios

Goal: Forecast short-term stock trends and quantify potential risk for better data-driven decision making.
